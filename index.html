About Me

My name is Thanh Doan but I usually go by Ted. I was born in Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam. I have always shown a strong passion for technology ever since I was little by messing with (and breaking) all pieces of tech I could get my hands on. I played around with all kinds devices growing up, Walkmans, MP3s, MP4s, DVD drives, and then computers. I would try to figure out all the functionalities of any device that I touched, changing all the system settings, themes, options. When I got my first computer, I know that I have met my match since there are so many things I could do and learn on the Internet. That is what got me into Computer Science and Software Engineering. I like solving problems, especially when I can solve problems with code.
